Fase 2
Design principles
Specific, Not Generic
Every choice has a reason. No placeholder language, no stock solutions. If you can swap the brand name and the text still works, it is not specific enough.
Structure First
Typography creates the architecture. Topol builds the structure, Apercu fills it with warmth. The hierarchy is always visible.
Pink, Never White
The background is always #FFE0DB. White exists only as letter color at display scale and as image frames. The pink is the brand.
Strictly Offline
The real experience happens in De Hallen. The brand guide creates materials that drive people to the physical space, not away from it.
